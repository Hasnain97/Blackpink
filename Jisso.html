Kim Jisso (김지수)

AGE: 25 (January 3, 1995)

DEBUT: Kim debuted as a member of Blackpink on August 8, 2016.

Kim Ji-soo better known mononymously as Jisoo, is a South Korean singer and actress. She made her debut as a vocalist in August 2016 as a member of the girl group Blackpink under YG Entertainment. She is the oldest member of the group. In addition to her native Korean, she is also able to speak Japanese and Chinese.

Kim Ji-soo was born in Gunpo, Gyeonggi, South Korea. She has an older brother and sister.Kim attended high school at the School of Performing Arts Seoul. In 2011, she joined YG Entertainment through auditions as a trainee.

Kim featured in several advertisements including Samsonite alongside actor Lee Min-ho, Smart Uniform, LG Electronics, and Nikon.
