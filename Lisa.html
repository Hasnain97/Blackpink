Lalisa Manoban (라리사 마노 반)

AGE: 23 (March 27, 1997)

DEBUT: Lisa debuted as one of four members of South Korean girl group Blackpink on August 2016

Lalisa Manoban (Thai: ปราณปรียา มโนบาล), better known by the mononym Lisa (리사), is a Thai rapper, singer and dancer based in South Korea as a member of the South Korean girl group Blackpink under YG Entertainment. She was the first non-Korean to debut under the agency. Lisa is multilingual. She speaks several languages including Thai, English, Korean, and basic Japanese and Chinese.

On November 5, 2018, she unveiled her YouTube channel, Lilifilm Official, which focuses on Travelling and Lifestyle. As of July 2019 she had over 1.3 million subscribers, and had received a YouTube Gold Play Button.

In April 2019, Lisa became the most followed K-pop idol on Instagram, with 17.4 million followers at the time. As of March 2020, Lisa maintains that status with 30.1 million followers on the platform.

On October 15, 2019, Lisa was announced to join the third installment of iQiyi's Chinese survival program Idol Producer as the dance mentor.

Through her work as a model for the South Korean cosmetics brand Moonshot, she became their brand ambassador in China on March 21, 2018. On March 28, 2019 Lisa signed her first solo endorsement deal for AIS Thailand, the largest GSM mobile phone operator in Thailand. Lisa became the highest paid brand presenter for AIS. Furthermore, her advertising campaign became the highest rated commercial in Thailand.

On May 11, 2019, Lisa became the brand presenter for the Samsung Galaxy S10 in Thailand. Her first promotional material for the brand was released on May 14.
